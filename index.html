About

Amigo Power Services provides hands-on project and site management within the power and energy sector. We support clients through all phases of delivery – from planning and execution to commissioning and handover.

With extensive field experience and an international project background, we focus on practical solutions, clear communication, and reliable delivery in complex technical environments.

Services

  • Project Management – Planning, coordination, and execution with focus on schedule, cost, and quality.
  • Site Management – On-site supervision, contractor coordination, and daily progress control.
  • Technical Inspections – Independent inspections, punch lists, and deviation reporting.
  • Commissioning Support – Testing, commissioning, and handover support.

Sectors

  • Power generation and distribution
  • Energy storage systems (BESS)
  • Critical infrastructure
  • Industrial electrical installations

About

Amigo Power Services delivers hands-on project and site management services within the power and energy sector. We support clients throughout the full project lifecycle – from planning and execution to testing and handover.

With extensive on-site experience and international project exposure, we focus on practical execution, technical accuracy, and clear communication in complex electrical environments.

Services

  • Project Management
    Planning, coordination, and delivery of electrical and power projects, with strong focus on schedule, cost control, and quality.
  • Site Management
    On-site supervision, contractor coordination, and daily progress control in high- and low-voltage environments.
  • Technical Inspections
    Independent inspections, punch lists, and deviation reporting for electrical installations and power systems.
  • Testing & Commissioning Support
    Support during testing, commissioning, and handover to ensure systems comply with technical and contractual requirements.

Sectors

  • Power generation and distribution
  • Electrical substations and grid infrastructure
  • Critical infrastructure
  • Industrial electrical installations
